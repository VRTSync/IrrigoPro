## Key Benefits of Monthly Consolidation

✅ **Single QuickBooks Invoice** - One invoice per customer per month instead of 10 separate invoices  
✅ **Complete Work History** - All December work consolidated into one comprehensive bill  
✅ **Simplified Tracking** - Easy to see total monthly activity and costs  
✅ **Professional Presentation** - Clean, organized billing for customers  
✅ **Efficient Processing** - Reduced administrative overhead  

---

## Technical Implementation

The system automatically:
- Identifies all completed work orders in the billing period
- Identifies all approved billing sheets in the billing period  
- Groups by customer and month
- Calculates consolidated totals with proper markup and tax
- Creates detailed line items tracking source (work order vs billing sheet)
- Generates unique monthly invoice numbers
- Prevents duplicate invoices for the same customer/period

This transforms billing from individual job invoices to comprehensive monthly statements - exactly what you need for streamlined QuickBooks management!
