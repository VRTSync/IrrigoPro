## QuickBooks Integration Benefits

### Single Monthly Entry
- **Before:** 10 separate invoice entries in QuickBooks
- **After:** 1 consolidated monthly invoice entry

### Customer View
- Clean, professional monthly statement
- Complete work history for the period
- Easy to understand billing breakdown
- Reference numbers for detailed tracking

### Accounting Benefits
- Simplified reconciliation
- Reduced data entry
- Better cash flow tracking
- Cleaner reporting

### QuickBooks Sync Fields
When synced to QuickBooks, the system sends:
- **Customer:** ABC Landscaping
- **Invoice Date:** January 5, 2025
- **Due Date:** February 4, 2025 (Net 30)
- **Line Items:** 10 detailed service entries
- **Subtotals:** Parts, Labor, Markup, Tax
- **Total:** $4,112.24
- **Memo:** "December 2024 Irrigation Services"

This format ensures your QuickBooks stays organized with one invoice per customer per month, while maintaining complete detail for tracking and customer transparency.
