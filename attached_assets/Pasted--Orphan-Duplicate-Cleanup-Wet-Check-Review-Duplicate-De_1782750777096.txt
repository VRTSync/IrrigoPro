## Fix 1 — Wet-check review: company_admin is on a STALE duplicate (highest priority)
The route `/wet-checks/:id/review` renders different components by role:
- `irrigation_manager` / `billing_manager` / `super_admin` → **`CombinedReviewPage`** (`pages/wet-checks/CombinedReviewPage.tsx`, current, modified 2026-06-16)
- **`company_admin` → `WetCheckReviewPage`** (`pages/wet-check-review.tsx`, older, 2026-06-03)
- `field_tech` → `WetCheckInspectionSummaryPage`

So **company_admin gets an older review page** than the other office roles and misses review work that shipped into `CombinedReviewSurface`/`CombinedReviewPage` (e.g. the zone-grouped inspection review).

### Steps
1. In `App.tsx`, change the **company_admin** switch (the `/wet-checks/:id/review` route, ~line 514) to render **`CombinedReviewPage`**, matching the manager/super_admin roles. (field_tech keeps `WetCheckInspectionSummaryPage` if that's intended for the tech summary.)
2. Confirm `CombinedReviewPage` renders correctly for a company_admin (role gating inside `CombinedReviewSurface` already covers admins/managers).
3. Once nothing references it, **delete `pages/wet-check-review.tsx`** (`WetCheckReviewPage`). Grep the repo first to confirm no other import/route uses it.
4. Verify parity: anything company_admin genuinely needed on the old page exists on `CombinedReviewPage` (it's the newer, fuller version — confirm before deleting).

### Acceptance
- A company_admin opening a wet-check review sees the **same** review surface as managers (zone-grouped inspection review included).
- `pages/wet-check-review.tsx` is deleted and unreferenced; no route renders it.
