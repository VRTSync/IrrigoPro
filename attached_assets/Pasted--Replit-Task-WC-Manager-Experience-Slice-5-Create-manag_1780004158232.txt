## Design Context (locked decisions)

Inherited from WC Manager Experience Slice 0.

**The role name is `irrigation_manager`.** Verified at `App.tsx:297`.
NOT `manager`. Every reference in this slice and downstream slices uses
`irrigation_manager`.

**The nav configs that exist:** `billingManagerNav`
(`nav-config.ts:104`), `superAdminNav` (`:166`), `companyAdminNav`
(`:203`). All three are wired in their respective App.tsx route blocks
via `<DesktopShell navConfig={...}>`. The irrigation manager block at
`App.tsx:297–356` renders `<Navigation />` directly without a config,
which is the root cause of the missing nav.

**`wetCheckGroup` is the shared sidebar group** at `nav-config.ts:87`.
It is already a member of the other three configs (lines 136, 189, 223
per the existing test at `nav-config.test.ts`). This slice adds it to
`managerNav` as well.

**The DesktopShell expects a `NavConfig`.** Its shape is defined at
`nav-config.ts:51` (`export interface NavConfig`). Adopt that interface
exactly; do not invent a parallel structure for the irrigation manager.

  configs.
