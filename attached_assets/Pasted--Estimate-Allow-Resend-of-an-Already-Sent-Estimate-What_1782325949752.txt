# Estimate â€” Allow Resend of an Already-Sent Estimate

**What & Why**
After "Email Customer," there's no way to **re-send** the estimate if the customer didn't receive it (bounce, spam, wrong address, etc.). Resend actually exists end-to-end â€” but it's **locked to expired estimates only**, which is the wrong trigger for the common "didn't get it" case.

**Current behavior:**
- **UI:** the **Resend** button renders only when the estimate is **expired** â€” `estimate-detail-modal.tsx:1264` (`{isExpired(estimate) && !isEstimateDeleted && â€¦}`). For a **sent but not-yet-responded** estimate, the only action offered is **"Copy approval link"** (`:1314`); Email Customer / Mark as Sent disappear after send (gated to `isPendingReview`, `:1336`/`:1347`).
- **Backend:** `POST /api/estimates/:id/resend` rejects anything not expired â€” `estimate-routes.ts:1657` (`if (estimate.lifecycleStatus !== "expired") â†’ 400 "Only expired estimates can be resent"`).

**Result:** to re-send to a customer who didn't receive it, you'd have to wait ~30 days for the token to expire. That's the gap.

**Fix:** allow resend for an **already-sent, not-yet-responded** estimate (status `sent` / internal `sent_to_customer`), in addition to expired â€” both the button and the endpoint. Keep the existing expired behavior. Exclude states where resend makes no sense (draft, approved, rejected, converted-to-WO).

Small slice. No schema change. The resend hook (`useEstimateResend`), confirm dialog (`ResendConfirmDialog`), and audit are already built â€” this just widens the gate.
