**What & Why**
When a field tech adds a Completion Photo while finishing a work order, the preview sometimes shows a **broken image** (the photo still uploads and saves â€” only the on-screen preview breaks, transiently).

**Root cause â€” a thumbnail timing race + a dropped local preview:**
1. The completion gallery (`FileUpload`) shows each photo by requesting the **server-generated `thumb` variant** of the image. Those variants are produced by the **finalize step, which is fire-and-forget** (not awaited) in `components/ui/file-upload.tsx`. So immediately after an upload, the `thumb` often **doesn't exist yet** â†’ the signed-URL/serve for the thumb fails â†’ broken image. Intermittent by nature: larger photos and **HEIC (iPhone)** take longer to process, so they break more often; a later refresh fixes it.
2. The completion screen hands photos back to the gallery as `{ url, fileName, originalName }` and **drops the local `previewUrl` blob** (`work-order-completion.tsx:1080`). That blob is the instant, always-available preview of the just-captured photo. Because it's stripped, the gallery has **no fallback** and must wait on the not-yet-ready server thumbnail.

Photo upload + save are fine; this is purely the preview. Frontend fix.

## Done looks like
- A just-uploaded completion photo shows its preview **immediately and reliably** (from the local blob), regardless of whether the server thumbnail has finished generating.
- No broken-image box on upload, including large/HEIC photos.
- Once the server variants exist, normal served thumbnails are used (e.g. on later views/refresh).

## Steps
1. **Preserve `previewUrl` through the completion flow.** In `work-order-completion.tsx` (~1080) where photos are mapped into `FileUpload`'s `files`, **include `previewUrl`**: `files={photos.map(p => ({ url: p.url, fileName: p.fileName, originalName: p.originalName, previewUrl: p.previewUrl }))}`. The `FileUpload.onFilesChange` result already returns `previewUrl` (`file-upload.tsx:350`) â€” keep it on the `CompletionUploadedFile` and pass it back so the gallery card uses the blob (`file-upload.tsx:872` prefers `previewUrl` when present). Confirm the same for the component's own preview list (~619â€“630).
2. **Don't revoke the blob too early.** Ensure the returned `previewUrl` blob isn't revoked while still displayed â€” it should live until the photo is removed or the form closes/submits (not on job auto-clear). Verify FileUpload's success-path job cleanup doesn't revoke the blob it just returned.
3. **Thumb â†’ base/medium fallback in `PhotoImage`.** When a `thumb` variant isn't available yet, the signed-URL/serve path should fall back to the **base/medium** image instead of returning a broken/404, so any surface showing a freshly-uploaded photo (not just this one) degrades gracefully. (Backend `getPhotoDownloadURL` already has a base fallback â€” make sure the **variant** request returns the base when the thumb is missing rather than failing, and/or have `PhotoImage` retry/relax to a larger variant on thumb error.)
4. (Optional, robustness) Have `PhotoImage` do a short one-time retry on a freshly-uploaded photo's thumb error, so even without the blob it self-heals within a second or two as finalize completes.
